India's First Maker Ship were Mentors become Makers along with Hardware Entrepreneurs An exclusive Makership from Brahm.Works which incubates only Hardware startups aims to achieve along with our “Make in India” campaign to make India a leader in manufacturing by 2030. Our Makership program provides an opportunity for hardware startups in various parts of the world to be part of this revolution. Having worked with more than 100 startups this new incubator model provides up to 10 Lakh rupees worth of credit to our selected startups. These credits can be later redeemed to be used in various services we provide. Having a collective experience of more than 100 years and being worked in Bay area in San Fransisco to shenzhen we provide Design, Innovation and Manufacturing capabilities to our selected startups. Our panel members does not consists of Mentors, It consists of Makers who can provide startups with services from Pixels to Patents.
